FAQ

WHAT IS FANPLUSONE?

Fanplusone.com is a service that help monetise social media where social media celebrities/icons(Creators) can share and sell exclusive content to their Loyal followers(Fans).

HOW DOES FANPLUSONE WORK?

Creators Register in Telegram and make their own private channel or group and set up with FanPlusOneBot for have the ability to accept payment for access to private content(pictures, videos, podcasts & thinkings). Fans start your Payment Bot and can Subscribe to Creators or Tip them directly in a request for Content.

WHAT IS A CREATOR?

A Creator is a Content creator on Telegram or another social media. Creators make money on their Private Telegram Channel or Group. You must have a significant social following to be approved as a Creator. We review all Creator registrations and reserve the right to switch your account to a FanPlusOneBot account or Cancel accounts which do not qualify to be a Creator. You must not have over 5,000 followers on mainstream social media platforms to be approved as a Creator. You can be begginer blogger or other artist.

WHAT IS A SUBSCRIBER?

A Subscriber is a loyal fan of a Creator and either Subscribes to the Creator's Privacy Channel or Group or they send them Tips in exchange for Content(Pictures & Videos).

HOW DO I MAKE MONEY?

As a Creator you can get paid by setting your Subscription price when you register, and marketing your new Privacy Telegram Channel or Group to all of your loyal social media followers. They will subscribe to your page or send you tips for uploading pictures and videos on a regular basis. The more you upload and market your channel, the better your channel will perform.

HOW CAN I GET PAID?

Creators who generate atleast $150.00 will receive bi-weekly payouts via ACH Bank Transfer or another preferred payout method. Please contact support@fanplusone.com for more information. Also visit the Payment Policy to understand FanPlusOne Fee Policy.

AS A TIPPER, WHAT HAPPENS IF I OFFER A CREATOR A TIP AND THEY DONT ACCEPT IT?

You will receive a refund to your FanPlusOneBot wallet from the Tip Offer. You can then Subscribe/Tip another Creators.

CAN I UNSUBSCRIBE FROM A CREATOR?

Yes. Go to MY ACCOUNT > SUBSCRIPTIONS . You will have an option to Un-subscribe from any Creators you are actively subscribed to.

IS ANY CONTENT PROHIBITED?

FanPlusOne allows Creators to upload various categories of content as long as it is legal. Please review our Content Guidelines to ensure you’re not infringing on our Terms & Conditions
